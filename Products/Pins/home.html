Our range of custom lapel pins is tailored to fit almost any size, shape, design, and color you have in mind. You can also choose from a variety of fasteners, from pins to magnets, to suit your preferences. These pins serve a variety of markets and uses.

If you're looking to promote your brand, we offer budget-priced, promotional quality give-aways. In the realm of corporate recognition, you can choose between hard enamel or precious metal pins to represent your company's achievements.

We also cater to those looking to commemorate years of service with pins adorned with diamonds or rhinestones. Alternatively, if you're an artist looking for merchandise to sell at concerts or a store seeking unique souvenirs, our custom pins make a compelling choice.

For the sports enthusiasts out there, we produce trading pins for baseball teams, creating a fun and memorable way to show team spirit.

Our production processes are facilitated by our domestic factory and numerous overseas partnerships, ensuring we can match your needs effectively. For a rustic feel, we offer zinc or pewter pins, cast in the USA. If you desire a design with multiple colors, our printed pins can deliver.

We also specialize in enamel pins, with options for hard enamel, soft enamel, or our budget soft enamel for a more cost-effective choice. If you're in search of a truly elegant option, we provide die-struck pins in brass or precious metals such as sterling silver or gold. And for an extra touch of luxury, we can incorporate rhinestones or diamonds into your pins.
