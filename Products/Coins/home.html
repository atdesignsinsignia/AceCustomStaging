We make Custom coins to suit your design, budget, logo, color scheme and event. We offer low minimum order quantities, so we can make from a few to many thousands of the same design. sorts of large and small events. We have produced a wide range of military medals for government and marine corps.

Military challenge coins are one of the most popular projects we create – coins for a squad, unit, platoon, company, battalion – any size. We have proudly worked with the brave men and women of the Air Force, Army, Marines, Navy, Coast Guard as well as First Responders. We ship domestically, overseas, and to APO, FPO, DPO.

We also make commemorative coins, memorial coins, religious icons, product tokens and wedding keepsakes. You can have them from about an inch up to about 6 inches – that is a big coin! Your custom coin can be enhanced with color, sculpted design or with mint-like edge treatments.
