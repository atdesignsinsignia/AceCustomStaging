Marathons, Triathlons, Running Events - Medals & Buckles

  • At AceCustom.com, we specialize in medals for Marathons, Triathlons, Ironman, 5K, 10K, Mud Runs, Zombie Runs & all sorts Running Events - both on a large and small scale.
  • Our medals and belt buckles are custom made - your logo, your race date, your design, your color, your ribbon color etc.
  • We can help you with the artwork and come up with a design for your special event.
  • Some event and race organizers will choose to get the same medals and ribbons for all finishers or participants.
  • We can also make some special ribbons or medals (different size, color, shape, text) for the top finishers
  • We can also provide age category medals which are of a different size, shape, or color, complete with labels or engraved plates showing how the participants finish in the race.
  • All medals can be provided with a selection of ribbon choices - including printed and sublimated (offshore and domestic).
  • If you have a sketch of what you want, that's great, just send it along
  • We have produced hundreds of thousands of marathon / triathlon medals and show about 300 below to give you an idea of what we can do for your race.
  • We can make medals for any type of an event - 5k, 10k, 15k, Trail racing, triathlons, team obstacle racing, mud run, color run, stair-climbing (tower building climb), ultra racing, ultra-marathon, Jingle-bell run, zombie themed run for your life, for those who get hungry while running - how about a food run (pizza, hot dog, donut)!
  • Jump Rings - Not included, please state if required.
  • Second side imprint / Union made designation available / Inside Cut Out / Coins - Without hoops / Copy Changes: Available at an additional charge.
  • Please also see a list of our standard ribbon colors.
  • To get started, give us some details of what you would like, and we'll get you a quote!
